Ingredients:
6 oz chicken
2 kaffir lime leaves
1 lemongrass stalk
1 1/2 cup coconut milk
4 sliced galangal
1 1/2 cup chicken stock
1/2 cup mushroom
1/3 cup fish sauce
1/3 cup lemon juice
3 1/2 tablespoon sugar
1/2 cup cilantro
1 fresh thai chili
2 tablespoon chili oil
1 green onion
optional seafood and tomatoes

Instructions:
Cook chicken and stock to boiling.  Add lime leaves, lemongrass, galangal, fish sauce, sugar, mushrooms, tomatoes, and seafood.  Boil until cooked.  Add coconut milk, lemon juice, chili, chili oil, green onion, and cilantro.
